About Noka

At Noka Matcha, we believe that great taste and well-being can go hand in hand. Founded by four friends with a shared love for matcha and minimalist design, our brand was born out of a desire to introduce matcha in a way that feels calm, clean, and comforting—just like a good cup of tea.

Why Noka?

The name Noka is inspired by the Japanese word 「農家」(pronounced nōkā), which means farmer. It reflects our appreciation for the natural roots of matcha and the hands that cultivate it. To us, Noka represents simplicity, nature, and a return to the essentials.

Our Mission

To serve thoughtfully crafted matcha drinks that balance flavor, health, and design—making each sip a moment of mindfulness and joy.

Our Vision

To be the go-to matcha lifestyle brand in Malaysia and beyond—celebrated for authenticity, creativity, and community.
