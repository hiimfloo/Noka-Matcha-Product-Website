Matcha Preparation Guide

@noka_matcha 🥄✨ How to Make the Perfect Matcha at Home ✨🍵 No fancy tools needed—just you, matcha, and a moment of calm. Whisk, sip, repeat 💚 #MatchaDIY #MatchaLover #NokaMatcha

Matcha

Mix ingredients below in a measuring cup with ASAGAO / Chasen whisk until creamy and no clumps:

a. Fresh Milk - 70mL or 1/3 cup
b. Granulated sugar: 2–3 tsp or 15mL syrup (use 60mL milk if sweetened)
c. REN / Matcha powder - 6g or 2 tsp + 1/4 tsp

Pour into a cup with 65mL milk and ice.

Houjicha

Combine Houjicha powder with sugar and 60mL milk using a milk frother or whisk.

a. Fresh Milk - 60mL
b. Houjicha powder - 6g or 2 tsp + 1/4 tsp
c. Sugar: 2 tsp (adjust to taste)

Top with 70mL iced milk for a nutty finish.

Genmaicha

Whisk genmaicha powder with sweetener and milk until smooth:

a. Fresh Milk - 60mL
b. Genmaicha powder - 6g or 2 tsp
c. Sugar syrup: 15mL or to taste

Pour over 65mL of cold milk and enjoy with ice.
