At Noka Matcha, we believe that great taste and well-being can go hand in hand. Founded by four friends with a shared love for matcha and mindful living, Noka is our way of blending flavor, simplicity, and intention into every cup.

“We didn’t just want to make a good matcha—we wanted to create a moment of calm you could return to every day. That’s the whole purpose of Noka.”

— Florence Ku, Creative Director at Noka.
